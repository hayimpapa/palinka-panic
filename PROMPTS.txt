================================================================================
PÁLINKA PANIC — FULL BUILD PROMPT
Week 17 of "52 apps in 52 weeks before I turn 52" by Hey I'm Papa
================================================================================

Build "Pálinka Panic" — a catching game featuring a Hungarian grandma at a
village market.

Standalone React app, own GitHub repo, deployable to Vercel.

CONCEPT:
Nagymama (Hungarian grandma) stands at the bottom of the screen. Pálinka
bottles fall from the top. You move her left and right to catch them before
they smash on the ground. The game gets faster and more chaotic. Warm,
folk-art illustrated style — think Hungarian embroidery meets cartoonish charm.

TECH STACK:
- React + Vite
- Canvas API for all game rendering
- No external image files — draw everything in code using Canvas 2D primitives
- Tailwind CSS for any UI outside the canvas
- Vercel-ready with vite.config.js

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONTROLS:
- Desktop: LEFT / RIGHT arrow keys, or A / D keys to move Nagymama
- Mobile: Touch and drag anywhere on screen, or tap left half / right half
- Game starts on first key press or tap

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAME MECHANICS:
- Bottles fall from random horizontal positions at the top
- Multiple bottles can be falling at once (starts with 1, increases over time)
- Nagymama has a tray/apron she holds out — this is the catch hitbox
- Catching a bottle = +10 points, +1 pálinka emoji in a counter top-left
- Bottle smashes on ground = lose 1 life (start with 3 lives shown as hearts)
- Game over when all 3 lives lost
- High score saved to localStorage

DIFFICULTY SCALING:
- Every 30 seconds: fall speed increases
- Every 50 points: one extra bottle added to simultaneous drops
- Every 100 points: bottles briefly wobble/spin making them harder to track
- Cap difficulty at 5 simultaneous bottles

SCORING:
- Catching streak bonus: 5 in a row = double points for next 10 catches
- Rare golden pálinka bottle (10% chance): worth 50 points and a cheer animation

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VISUALS — draw ALL characters and objects using Canvas 2D drawing primitives
only. No emoji substitutes for main characters. Draw every detail explicitly as
shapes, arcs, lines, and fills.

NAGYMAMA (player character):
Body:
- Short and round body shape, warm and sturdy — a grandma, not a sprite
- Wide A-line skirt, dark navy blue, reaching below the knee
- White apron over the skirt with simple red embroidery pattern along the hem
  (small X-stitch diamonds, drawn as small filled red diamond shapes)
- Long-sleeved blouse in cream/off-white
Head and face:
- Round face, soft peach skin tone
- Small round eyes with laugh lines (two curved lines beside each eye)
- A warm smile — small curved mouth, slightly open showing top teeth
- Rosy cheeks: two soft pink filled circles
- White hair in a neat bun on top of head (layered arc shapes)
- Dark headscarf tied under the chin (triangular shape over the bun, polka-dot
  pattern in dark blue)
Arms and tray:
- Short arms outstretched to the sides
- She holds a wide wooden tray/board in both hands — this is the catch zone
- Tray: long rectangle, warm brown, slight 3D depth effect, darker bottom edge
- When catching: brief arm-raise animation (tray lifts 8px upward for 200ms)
Shoes:
- Black flat shoes, simple rounded rectangle shapes

ANIMATIONS:
Idle: gentle breathing — body scales vertically by 1-2% on a 2s sine loop
Walk: alternating leg arcs, 4-frame cycle when moving
Catch success: arms raise, tray up, mouth becomes a big O, 6 yellow stars burst
  and fade over 400ms
Life lost: front-facing face, frustrated V eyebrows, flat downturned mouth, arms
  drop, background flashes red 300ms, resets after 1 second
Golden bottle catch: both arms raised in triumph, "Egészségre!" speech bubble,
  20-rectangle confetti burst

PÁLINKA BOTTLES:
Standard: tall narrow glass bottle, light-blue semi-transparent glass, amber
  liquid fill in lower 60%, round cork, cream "PÁLINKA" label with tiny apricot
  and leaf, white shine highlight; gentle ±5° wobble (±15° past 100 points)
Smash: bottle vanishes, 6-8 irregular shards scatter, 4-5 amber teardrop
  splashes spray up, fade over 500ms
Golden (10%): gold metallic fill, rotating shimmer highlight, sparkle stars

BACKGROUND:
- Sky: warm gradient, pale yellow to soft peach
- Village market stalls (rectangles + red/white striped triangle roofs)
- Hungarian-colour bunting (red/white/green triangular flags on a curved rope)
- Rolling green hills silhouette
- Terracotta cobblestone ground (offset grid lines)

SOUND (Web Audio API only, no files):
- Catch: warm sine "clink" ~440Hz, 80ms
- Smash: noise burst + descending tone, 300ms
- Golden: ascending C-E-G arpeggio, 150ms each
- Game over: slow descending tone with reverb

UI ELEMENTS:
- Score: top centre, large bold, dark red (#8B0000)
- High score: top right, smaller
- Lives: top left, 3 red heart icons (drawn as Canvas hearts)
- Pálinka counter: small bottle icon beside total caught

START SCREEN:

- Subtitle: "Catch the bottles before Nagymama cries! 🧓🍶"
- Animated Nagymama in idle breathing loop
- "Tap or press any key to start"

GAME OVER SCREEN:
- "Ó jaj!" large, final + high score, sad front-facing Nagymama, red "Play
